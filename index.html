About Me

Hello! My name is Lucas, and I am a learning full stack developer located out of Boulder, Colorado. I focus on writing clean, reliable, and efficient code. When I'm not busy coding, I rock climb, bike, and run a DnD group.

By the end of my boot camp, I will be adept in all aspects of full stack developing and have completed three group projects.
Outside of the programming world, I have managed multiple teams and stores, and completed seminars and classes teaching leadership qualities. These skills give me a welcome advantage in working with project teams, which I will bring with me into my new career.
